Diljit Dosanjh

Diljit Dosanjh is a renowned Indian singer, actor, and entertainer celebrated for his contributions to Punjabi music and cinema. Known for his soulful voice and hit tracks like Do You Know and 5 Taara, he has also delivered iconic performances in films like the Jatt and Juliet series and Honsla Rakh. With his unique blend of traditional Punjabi roots and contemporary style, Diljit has become a global icon, bridging cultures through his music and acting.
