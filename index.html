Dosanjh Beats

Dosanjh Beats is your gateway to discovering artists, exploring their top albums, and uncover to their best tracks. But it doesn’t stop there - this platform empowers you to add your own favorite artists, albums, and tracks to the mix.


Founded by MT, I created Dosanjh Beats to celebrate the universal power of music and to provide a platform where fans and artists can connect through their shared love for creativity. The platform allows users to explore, showcase, and contribute to a growing catalog of artists, albums, and tracks.
