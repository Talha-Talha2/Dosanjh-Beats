Dosanjh Beats allows you to view artists, their top albums as well as their bst tracks. You can also add your own artists, albums and tracks. Dosanjh Beats was created to make a platform where everyones music can be seen and hence discovered by everyone, allowing users to include their own favourites.


Founded by MT Dosanjh Beats was created by him as a tribute to his favurite artist 'Diljit Dosanjh' and his passion for punjabi music to be raised to the highest platfoms available.
